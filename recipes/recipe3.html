No-Bake Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

(go back to homepage)

Description

These irresistible no-bake bars combine the classic pairing of chocolate and peanut butter in a smooth, layered treat with a hint of crunch. They're quick to make, wonderfully decadent, and chill beautifully â€” making them a perfect, fuss-free dessert to end any meal on a sweet note

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups of powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 of cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 1/2 of cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix peanut butter, melted butter, powdered sugar, and graham cracker crumbs until smooth.
  2. Press mixture evenly into a parchment-lined 8x8 inch (20x20 cm) baking dish.
  3. Melt chocolate chips (in a microwave or double boiler) and spread over the peanut butter layer.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until firm.
  5. Cut into bars and serve chilled.
